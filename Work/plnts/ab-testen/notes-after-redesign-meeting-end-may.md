# Why do we need A-B testing?
One of the hurdles we came across during our redesign meetings, is that we all have different opinions.
We all have different ideas of what the new website should look like. We all have different ideas of what the new website should do.
It's hard to come to a consensus when we all have different ideas.
A-B testing is a way to test our ideas. It's a way to see what works and what doesn't.
