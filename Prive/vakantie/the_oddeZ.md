Title: "The OddeZ"

A Gen Z Play of the Odyssey

Characters:

- \textcolor{cyan}{\textbf{Odysseus}: The hero of the story}
- \textcolor{red}{\textbf{Athena}: The goddess who gives questionable advice}
- \textcolor{blue}{\textbf{Poseidon}: The grumpy god of the sea}
- \textcolor{magenta}{\textbf{Circe}: The sorceress who loves making magical smoothies}
- \textcolor{purple}{\textbf{Penelope}: Odysseus's ever-patient wife}
- \textcolor{violet}{\textbf{Sirens}: Enchanting singers with a dangerous allure}
- \textcolor{olive}{\textbf{Zeus}: The king of the gods}
- \textcolor{brown}{\textbf{Polyphemus}: The one-eyed giant Cyclops}

---

- **Lotus-Eaters**: Peaceful but forgetful people}
- **Aeolus**: The god of the winds
- \textcolor{gray}{\textbf{Suitors}: A bunch of goofy guys competing for Penelope's attention}
- \textcolor{black}{\textbf{Narrator}: The amazing, wonderful and modest storyteller}

- And ofcorse the Crew, which are you, the audience! 

\newpage
# The Departure

Narrator: (jumping in excitement) Welcome, everyone, to the epic, the legendary, highkey  slay of a play embarking on the journey of Odysseus! Our hero is ready to set sail from Troy back to Ithaca. But, oh boy, does he have a journey ahead!

\textcolor{cyan}{\textbf{Odysseus}: (posing dramatically) Farewell, Troy! Ithaca, here I come! Nothing can stop me! I’m about to secure the bag and go home!}

\textcolor{red}{\textbf{Athena}: (appearing with a burst of confetti, and proceeds to step through it)  Hold your horses, Odysseus! I've got some, uh, helpful advice. Always take the scenic route, and never, ever say no to a party!} 

\textcolor{cyan}{\textbf{Odysseus}: (confused but enthusiastic) Scenic routes and parties? Bet! (start a TikTok dance)}

# Zeus’s Vision

Narrator: Meanwhile, on Mount Olympus, Zeus starts tripping.

\textcolor{olive}{\textbf{Zeus}: (holding his head) Whoa, I just had the craziest vision! I saw the child of Hector slaying!}

\textcolor{red}{\textbf{Athena}: Yo, Zeus! You good? What did you see?}

\textcolor{olive}{\textbf{Zeus}: (dramatically) I saw the child of Hector slaying dragons, conquering kingdoms, and totally owning the battlefield. It was lit!}

\textcolor{red}{\textbf{Athena}: (rolling her eyes) Zeus, you've been watching too much TikTok. That was probably just a skibidi vision. Chill, old man.}

\textcolor{olive}{\textbf{Zeus}: (confused) What's Skibidi?}

\textcolor{red}{\textbf{Athena}: Skibideez nuts!}

# The Lotus-Eaters

Narrator: Odysseus and his crew then arrive at the land of the Lotus-Eaters, where everyone seems baked out of their minds. The Lotus-Eaters say, "Welcome! Have some lotus flowers!"

Narrator: As one of the crew members shout "This is bussin'!", and they start eating the lotus flowers and they turn into a bunch of spaced-out hippies.

\textcolor{cyan}{\textbf{Odysseus}: (shouting towards his crew) Snap out of it! We need to go home!}

\textcolor{red}{\textbf{Athena}: (appearing out of thin air) Odysseus, stop wasting time! You've got to stay focused!}

\textcolor{cyan}{\textbf{Odysseus}: (pulling his crew back to the ship) No cap, Athena. Let's bounce!}

# The Cyclops

Narrator: And so, Odysseus and his crew land on an island. But what's this? A one-eyed giant? Enter Polyphemus, the Cyclops!

\textcolor{brown}{\textbf{Polyphemus}: (Make a illuminati triangle on 4head) Who dares enter me casa?}

\textcolor{cyan}{\textbf{Odysseus}: (smirking) It's me, Nobody!}

\textcolor{brown}{\textbf{Polyphemus}: (scratching his head) Nobody? Well, Nobody, prepare to be my dinner fam!}

\textcolor{cyan}{\textbf{Odysseus}: (winking to the audience) Time for a little trickery! (holds up a bottle of "Sleepy-Time Milk") Hey, Cyclops! How about a drink?}

\textcolor{brown}{\textbf{Polyphemus}: (gulps it down and snores loudly)}

\textcolor{cyan}{\textbf{Odysseus}: (sneaking away) I'm the GOAT, get rekt giant! Ez clap!}

Narrator: On their way out of the cave, Odysseus and his crew have a little fun with the Cyclops.

\textcolor{cyan}{\textbf{Odysseus}: (taunting) Hey, Cyclops! Guess what? I’m Odysseus, and I just yeeted your eye!}

# Aeolus and the Bag of Winds

Narrator: After their adventure with the Cyclops, Odysseus the bag of winds from Aeolus to help him sail home. Aeolus, the god of the winds, is ready to assist.

Narrator: As Aeolus hands over the bag he says "Here, Odysseus. This bag contains all the winds, including my own personal wind. Release them wisely, and they will guide you home."

\textcolor{cyan}{\textbf{Odysseus}: (gratefully) Sheesh, thank you, Aeolus!}

# Poseidon's Wrath

Narrator: As Odysseus sails away, Poseidon, the god of the sea, is furious with him for blinding his son, Polyphemus.

\textcolor{blue}{\textbf{Poseidon}: (appearing with a splash) Odysseus, I don't care that you give main character vibes, you blinded my son Polyphemus! Now you will face my wrath!}

\textcolor{cyan}{\textbf{Odysseus}: (making angry L signs towards Poseidon) Oh, come on, Poseidon!You are lowkey messing up the vibes. It was just a prank, bro!}

\textcolor{blue}{\textbf{Poseidon}: (smirking) Get ready to be yeeted across the seas!}

Narrator: Poseidon sends storms to toss Odysseus's ship around like a toy boat. The crew starts panicking and all hope seems lost.

\textcolor{cyan}{\textbf{Odysseus}: (holding the bag of winds) Wait! I’ve got this!}

Narrator: Odysseus opens the bag slightly, and the winds guide them out of the storm)

\textcolor{blue}{\textbf{Poseidon}: (furious) I may have taken the L this time, Odysseus, but I'll be back to claim the W!}

# Circe’s Island

Narrator: After narrowly escaping Poseidon's wrath, Odysseus finds himself on Circe’s crib.

\textcolor{magenta}{\textbf{Circe}: Welcome, weary travelers! How about some `Thirst Trap Cotton Candy Dream Smoothies`?}

\textcolor{cyan}{\textbf{Odysseus}: (taking a sip) This is delicious! What’s in it?}

\textcolor{magenta}{\textbf{Circe}: (giggling) Oh, just a dash of this and a pinch of that... and maybe a sprinkle of pig potion!}

Narrator: The potion turns the crew into pigs as they start squealing and oinking.

\textcolor{cyan}{\textbf{Odysseus}: (facepalming) Big yikes! Not again!}

\textcolor{red}{\textbf{Athena}: Chill, Odysseus! Just throw a karaoke party, and she’ll turn them back.}

\textcolor{cyan}{\textbf{Odysseus}: (grabbing a mic) Hit it! (start singing terribly off-key)}

\textcolor{magenta}{\textbf{Circe}: (covering her ears) Okay, okay! I’ll turn them back! Just stop singing!}

# The Sirens

Narrator: Sailing onward, Odysseus and his crew encounter the Sirens, whose rizz lure sailors to their doom.

\textcolor{violet}{\textbf{Sirens}: (rizzing alluringly) Come to us, Odysseus! We’ve got bangers for days!}

Narrator: Odysseus has a plan to resist the Sirens' call. He orders his crew to tie him to the mast and block their ears with wax.

\textcolor{cyan}{\textbf{Odysseus}: (tied to the mast) Crew, whatever you do, don’t listen to these sus E-girls and E-boys! Keep rowing!}

Narrator: The crew starts rowing furiously, and Odysseus is unable to resist the Sirens' call.

\textcolor{violet}{\textbf{Sirens}: (singing) You can't resist me, Odysseus! I'm lit AF!}

Narrator: Odysseus is mesmerized by the Sirens' song, but in the end, they sail away to safety.

# The Suitors

Narrator: Finally, after many more cringe-worthy adventures, Odysseus returns home. But what’s this? A bunch of suitors trying to flex on Penelope?

Narrator: One of the suiters comes forth and says "Penelope, your vibe is so strong, it’s giving my heart major FOMO!"

\textcolor{purple}{\textbf{Penelope}: (rolling her eyes) Seriously? Get a life!}

\textcolor{cyan}{\textbf{Odysseus}: (bursting in) Honey, I’m home!}

\textcolor{purple}{\textbf{Penelope}: (sarcastically) Took you long enough!}

\textcolor{cyan}{\textbf{Odysseus}: (striking a pose) Did you miss me?}

\textcolor{purple}{\textbf{Penelope}: (sighing) Just a bit. Now, let’s kick these clowns out.}

Narrator: And so, Odysseus and Penelope are reunited at last. Cue the epic, fortnite ending dance!

All Characters: (dancing)

Narrator: And that, folks, is the cringey, epic tale of Odysseus! Thanks for joining us on this ridiculous journey! TLDR they all lived happily ever after.

---

Curtain closes.
